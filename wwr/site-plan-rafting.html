Overview

Purpose

We’re a professional team that provide guides dedicated to leading trips down some of the most beautiful and fun-filled rivers in the world. Our most popular trips are the River Niger and The River Benue in Middle belt Nigeria, but we also offer a few special trips to places we love and know you will too. And we are a safe place to tour the water ways immersed in a heart lifting fun time with family and loved ones...

Audience

Whether it's your first on the river or you are a seasoned river veteran, our guides and staff are there to make sure you have the trip you'll always fantasize about.From falling asleep beneath the stars at night to enjoying the refreshing splash of rapids on a hot day, we’ll take care of the details and leave you free to explore. We take you on type of trips, focused on amazing adventures, healthy food, and creating good memories. Our staff and guides are excited that you have picked us to join you for your vacation on the river.
